Chocolate Cake

2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups sugar
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

2 cups cold water
2/3 cup canola oil
1 tablespoon vanilla
2 teaspoons white vinegar

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
Mix dry ingredients into large bowl.
In seperate bowl, mix wet ingredients.
Slowly stir wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until fully wet. It will look thin and that is okay.
Pour the batter into greased pans and bake about 30 minutes.  Test centre with toothpick that it comes out dry.

For cupcakes, the baking time is 24 to 26 minutes.

Cool for 10 minutes

known substitutions:
equivalent, melted, unsweetened chocolate squares
equivalent sweetener such as cane, honey, raw, agave (may change flavour slightly)
equivalent butter, margerine, or other oild (also may slightly change flavour)
