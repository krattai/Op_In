General notes and thoughts going forward with OpIn:

OpIn would consist of information that may or may not be useful to everyone or even anyone, yet it is intended to be a knowledge resource in the event that it may be useful at some point in the future for someone or some group.

Economic value could very well remain a constant challenge as value is a perception.  Conversations and efforts made towards economic advancement might best be served as a how it benefits things especially based on needs, rather than wants.
